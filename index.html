About This Website:

This site was created with the sole purpose of teaching others about the game Syobon Action and how to compile it using Emscripten. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly and accessible platform for anyone interested in this unique game, whether they are complete beginners or experienced developers.

Prior to the establishment of this website, there was no centralized resource that provided comprehensive information about Syobon Action. This gap in available information made it difficult for newcomers to understand the game's mechanics, development processes, and the tools needed to work with it. Recognizing this need, our dedicated team set out to create a multilayered resource that not only covers the basics but also delves into more advanced topics related to the game.

On this site, our tutorials and guides are designed to be straightforward and supportive, guiding users through every step of the process, from the initial setup to the final compilation. We hope that by providing clear instructions and helpful tips, we can empower users to experiment with the game and even contribute to its development in new and exciting ways. Additionally, if you are looking to seek assistance with an issue you've encountered along the way, feel free to contact us by clicking the Help/Support link in the toolbar above!
